A GLOBAL ALLIANCE TO REGENERATE OCEANS FROM THE ISLANDS UP

OIRA is a non-profit alliance working with island and coastal communities to regenerate ocean ecosystems and strengthen resilience through clean maritime mobility, circular economies, and inclusive governance.

Maritime activities: key to island survival, threat to ecosystems when mismanaged

Maritime activities are the key to island subsistence and development, but when poorly managed, they directly threaten the ecosystems on which community survival depends. Only 3% of marine protected areas benefit from effective protection with real management plans.
