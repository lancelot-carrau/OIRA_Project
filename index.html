A GLOBAL ALLIANCE TO REGENERATE OCEANS FROM THE ISLANDS UP

OIRA is a non-profit alliance working with island and coastal communities to regenerate ocean ecosystems and strengthen resilience through clean maritime mobility, circular economies, and inclusive governance.

"Islands face a mobility paradox: the same systems that sustain life today are driving collapse tomorrow. OIRA is building the solution."
